Projects

Project 1

CheckBags

CheckBags is a luggage dropshipping service designed to simplify travel. Customers book online, providing their arrival airport, date, and desired destination. CheckBags partners with local couriers to pick up luggage from the customer's location and deliver it to their chosen address. This service offers travelers convenience, time-saving, and peace of mind by eliminating the hassle of carrying luggage throughout their journey. CheckBags leverages technology to streamline the booking process and manage logistics, creating a seamless experience for both customers and businesses..

Project 2

Foody

This website, called "Foody," is designed to be a captivating online dining experience. The homepage immediately sets the tone with the tagline "It's not just food, it's an experience." This is further emphasized by the high-quality image of a beautifully plated pasta dish, inviting users to explore the menu. The website offers a 5% discount for first-time orders, encouraging new customers to try the restaurant. Users can easily view the menu or book a table directly from the homepage. Additionally, the website displays customer reviews, showcasing positive feedback and building trust with potential diners.

Project 3

FeedBack FORM

This project presents a user-friendly feedback form with fields for Name, Email, Subject, and a detailed message. The form is connected to a backend system, enabling secure data storage, automated processing, and potential user management. This integration enhances the functionality of the form by allowing for data analysis, report generation, and controlled access to feedback information. The form's clean design and backend support make it a valuable tool for gathering user insights and improving services or products based on collected feedback. Future enhancements could include features like a rating system, file uploads, customizable fields, and an analytics dashboard to further enhance its capabilities.
